The Best Movies According to Tousif

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers: Infinity War

"Avengers: Infinity War" brings together nearly every major Marvel hero to face their greatest threat yet: Thanos. The Mad Titan seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones to wipe out half of all life in the universe, believing it's the only way to save it from overpopulation. The film is a desperate race against time as the heroes attempt to stop his devastating plan, leading to a shocking and impactful ending.

Avengers: End Game

"Avengers: Endgame" picks up after the devastating events of "Infinity War," with the surviving heroes grappling with their immense loss. They embark on a desperate mission to undo Thanos's snap, traveling through time to gather the Infinity Stones. The film culminates in an epic final battle against Thanos and his army, bringing an emotional and definitive conclusion to the Infinity Saga.

Iron Man

"Iron Man" introduces the world to the brilliant, billionaire industrialist Tony Stark, a weapons manufacturer who has an unexpected change of heart. After being gravely injured and held captive by terrorists, he builds a rudimentary armored suit to escape. Upon returning home, Stark dedicates himself to perfecting his suit and using it to fight injustice, ultimately revealing his secret identity to the world.
